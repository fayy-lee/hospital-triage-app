About the Application

This web application helps hospital staff and patients understand wait times in the emergency room. It includes an administrator panel for managing patient information and a patient portal for checking approximate wait times.

Features

  • Administrator panel for adding and managing patients.
  • Patient portal for checking wait times based on name and code.
  • Real-time updates for patient information and wait times.
